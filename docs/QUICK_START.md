# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with VoyagrIQ in 10 minutes!

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (2 minutes)

1. Go to **[voyagriq.com](https://voyagriq.com)**
2. Click **"Get Started"**
3. Choose your plan:
   - **Start**er: Solo advisor, $49/mo
   - **Standard**: Growing team, $99/mo (most popular!)
   - **Premium**: Established agency, $199/mo
4. Enter your email and create a password
5. Add payment info (14-day free trial on Starter/Standard)
6. Click **"Start Free Trial"**

‚úÖ **You're in!** You'll see the dashboard with sample data.

---

## Step 2: Set Your Default Commission (1 minute)

This is your standard commission rate that applies to all trips (unless you override it).

1. Click **"Settings"** in the navigation
2. Click **"Agency Settings"**
3. Enter your commission rate (e.g., **15%**)
4. Click **"Save"**

üí° **Tip**: You can set different rates for different client types later!

---

## Step 3: Add Your First Trip (3 minutes)

Let's track a real trip to see how it works.

###  Method 1: Quick Add (Fastest)

1. Click **"Add Trip"** in the top navigation
2. Fill in the basics:
   - **Client Name**: John Doe
   - **Destination**: Paris, France
   - **Departure**: 2026-06-15
   - **Return**: 2026-06-22
   - **Travelers**: 2
   - **Base Cost**: $5,000
3. Click **"Save Trip"**

**What happens**: VoyagrIQ automatically calculates your commission using your default rate (15% of $5,000 = $750).

### Method 2: Detailed Entry (More Info)

1. Go to **"Trips"** page
2. Click **"New Trip"**
3. Fill in all the details:
   - Client info
   - Trip dates and destination
   - Costs and vendors
   - Add-ons and fees
4. Click **"Save"**

**Result**: Trip is saved and appears in your dashboard and analytics!

---

## Step 4: Check Your Dashboard (1 minute)

1. Click **"Dashboard"** (or the VoyagrIQ logo)
2. See your trip displayed:
   - **Total Revenue**: $750 (your commission)
   - **Average Commission**: $750 per trip
   - **Total Trips**: 1
   - **Top Destinations**: Paris, France

3. Click around:
   - **Trip list**: See all trips
   - **Charts**: Visual breakdown
   - **Filters**: View by date, client, destination

---

## Step 5: Try Analytics (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Analytics"** in navigation
2. See charts showing:
   - Revenue over time
   - Top destinations
   - Top clients
   - Vendor spending
3. Change date range to see different views
4. Click on charts to drill down

üí° **Tip**: Add more trips to see trends and patterns!

---

## Step 6: Export Your First Report (1 minute)

1. Go to **"Trips"** page
2. Click **"Export"** button
3. Choose format:
   - **CSV**: Opens in Excel
   - **Excel**: Formatted spreadsheet (Standard+)
   - **PDF**: Professional report (Standard+)
4. Click **"Download"**

**Result**: Your trip data in a familiar format!

---

## What's Next?

### Week 1: Get Your Data In

**Import past trips** (Standard+ only):
1. Go to **"Data"** ‚Üí **"Bulk Import"**
2. Download our CSV template
3. Fill in your trips
4. Upload and import

**Or add trips manually**:
- Add 5-10 recent trips
- This gives you data to analyze

### Week 2: Set Up Pricing Rules

**Vendor Pricing** (Standard+):
1. Go to **"Settings"** ‚Üí **"Vendor Pricing"**
2. Add rules for your top 5 vendors
3. Example: "Hilton Hotels: 10% markup"

**Client Overrides** (Premium+):
1. Go to **"Settings"** ‚Üí **"Client Pricing"**
2. Add overrides for VIP clients
3. Example: "John Smith: 12% commission"

### Week 3: Review & Optimize

1. **Check Analytics**: What's working?
2. **Adjust Pricing**: Based on your data
3. **Create Reports**: Share with your team

### Week 4: Automate

**Scheduled Reports** (Standard+):
1. Go to **"Settings"** ‚Üí **"Scheduled Reports"**
2. Get weekly/monthly reports automatically

**API Integration** (Premium+):
1. Connect your booking software
2. Auto-import trips
3. See [API Docs](API_REFERENCE.md)

## Common Tasks

### Add Another Trip
1. Click **"Add Trip"**
2. Fill in details
3. Save

### Edit a Trip
1. Go to **"Trips"**
2. Click on the trip
3. Make changes
4. Click **"Update"**

### Change Your Commission Rate
1. Go to **"Settings"** ‚Üí **"Agency Settings"**
2. Update rate
3. Save
4. New trips use new rate (old trips unchanged)

### Invite Team Members (Standard+)
1. Go to **"Settings"** ‚Üí **"Team"**
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter email
4. Choose role (Admin/User)
5. Send invite

### Upgrade Your Plan
1. Go to **"Account"** ‚Üí **"Subscription"**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select new plan
4. Confirm
5. Takes effect immediately!

## Tips for Success

### ‚úÖ Do This

- **Enter trips right away**: After you book them
- **Check dashboard weekly**: Spot trends early
- **Use categories**: Group trips by type
- **Export regularly**: Backup your data
- **Review pricing**: Adjust based on analytics

### ‚ùå Avoid This

- **Don't wait to import**: Enter trips as you go
- **Don't ignore analytics**: They show what's working
- **Don't over-complicate**: Start simple, add features as needed
- **Don't skip backups**: Export your data monthly
- **Don't forget passwords**: Use a password manager
